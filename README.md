## 📖 Story in 30 seconds

I started freelancing in **2009** as a student at Gobelins — auto-entrepreneur under my own name, web and Flash projects on weekends to fund my studies and ship my first ideas. That never closed.

- **2013–2017** · Deep dive at Magency/Sparkup — enterprise iOS for **Renault, Nissan, CHANEL, LVMH, EDF, SNCF, Disney**
- **2018** · iOS Evangelist at 3W Academy (Jan-Apr) → met Faustin Veyssiere → sport-app R&D begins (Blast → Carbon → Maya.fit) · featured on **France 2 / Télématin** for iOS development education during the bootcamp
- **2020** · Brief Lead iOS at Prisma Media (4 apps, 8M+ MAU)
- **2021–2022** · Iziwork (1.7M users) — MVVM + Coordinator migration
- **2024** · HelloAsso freelance → founded **OBYW.one** to formalize 15 years of independent work
- **Nov 2024 – Jun 2025** · Baby Sittor CDI · SPM modularization, KMM, Live Activities
- **Jun 2025 → today** · Full-time on OBYW.one — **Shikki** is the headline build
